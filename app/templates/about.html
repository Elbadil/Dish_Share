The Inspiration Behind Dish Share

Dish Share was born from my love for coding and cooking. Imagine it as a tasty blend of tech and taste. As a student at ALX, I wanted to prove my coding chops by building something real and delicious. So, I set out to create a web app from scratch. Think Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript - the whole coding kitchen. Dish Share is the result, where every line of code is like crafting a perfect recipe. It's not just about sharing recipes, it's about sharing the journey of turning code into something practical and user-friendly. Come join me at the intersection of coding and culinary passion.
